description

Calabar Harbour Resort & Spa was established in 2014, and has since been dedicated to ensuring we are one of the top hotels in Calabar and beyond. At Calabar Harbour Resort & Spa, there are only 13 rooms and 3 suites and we are committed to exceeding your expectation while making each guest feel like royals.
Each of our rooms and suites are very comfortable, all with beautiful furniture, fabrics and decoration, TV, cable, Wi-fi and new anti-stress beds ensuring you a restful stay. Our large Suite room has a kitchen area, a fabulous sunken bath tub, and breathtaking landscape scenery view from over the windows. Our hotel can be great for single travelers, families, and couples and is also ideal for groups. Perhaps if you are a group traveling to Calabar for a wedding, volunteer project or passing through in a travel tour group this is the hotel for you! We have 16 beds with total capacity of 32 people. The hotel and event center of Calabar HarbourResort & Spa is extremely popular for weddings and other celebrations here in Calabar.
